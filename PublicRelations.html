Public Outreach

As an organization, Engineering House is very involved in the Rochester community. We feel that it is important to give back to the people that help to support us. Every year, our members participate in many outreach programs, including:

  • Ronald McDonald House

  • Foodlink

  • Margret's House

  • RIT Library

  • Friendly Home

  • 5k Participation

  • Goodbye-Goodbuy!

  • Animal Shelter

  • Flower City Pickers

  • Community Garden

  • Red Cross Blood Drives

  • Relay for Life
